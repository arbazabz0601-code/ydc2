What is Off-Page SEO?

Off-Page SEO refers to all the activities done outside your website that impact your search rankings. The most crucial element is backlink building — earning links from other high-authority websites that point to your site. These links tell Google that your website is trustworthy, relevant, and deserves to rank higher.

But Off-Page SEO is more than just links. It includes brand mentions, online reputation management, guest blogging, social media signals, and influencer outreach — all of which create digital credibility.
